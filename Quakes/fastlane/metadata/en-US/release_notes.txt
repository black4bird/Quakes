Some bugs were discovered, but fear not they have been annihilated!

• Did you feel it?
You are now able to see the number of people said they felt a quake. If you felt the quake you are also able to fill out a short form to indicate when and how you felt the quake.

• Filter the map
You can now see which quakes happened when. Using a slider you can adjust how many quakes happened through out the month.
